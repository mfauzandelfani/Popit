What's Pop it?

Pop it is a toy in the form of a piece of silicone like bubbles that we can squeeze. These toys also come in different shapes, colors, and sizes. One example, this pop it toy is available in the form of a unicorn and ice cream. To play it, we can squeeze it in and then flip it over, then do it again. This toy is not only entertaining but also can train motor and sensory early childhood.

Explenation

This toy is in the form of silicone like bubble wrap or bubble wrap which is usually used to wrap packages. Your little one can press the bubbles on this pop it toy, then the bubbles will reappear. How to play is the first step as before, which is to determine the number of players. determine the turn of the player from the number of players themselves. Prepare marbles as the main ingredient and of course pop it itself. Drop the marbles on the pop it, then flip it over. The first player starts to press the pop it bubble and is followed by the second, third, etc. until they find a marble. The winning rule is the player who finds or presses the bubble pop it containing marbles.
